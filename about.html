Description of the project

Once upon a time, 6 talented Computer Science students had to develop a website for their Software Product Engineering module from University of Bristol. In order to complete this task, and fulfil the clients' need, the students joined their forces.

The purpose of the website is to create a system for managing the volunteers from Bristol City Council Museums, Art Galleries and Archives.
